Final Class Projects Visual Analytics Course

Here is a list of the Visual Analytics projects developed during our Fall 2019 Course at Los Andes University. Each group was tasked to develop a Visual Analytics tool to help solve a real problem from a real user. The students successfully worked through the problems of obtaining and cleaning real data, and more importantly extracting real value from them.
